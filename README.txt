### Rosary notes

1. sign of the cross
2. Holding the Crucifix, say the Apostles' Creed.
3. On the first bead, say an Our Father.
4. Say one Hail Mary on each of the next three beads. (for faith, hope, and charity)
5. Say the Glory Be

For each of the five decades, 
6. announce the Mystery (perhaps followed by a brief reading from Scripture) 
7. large bead - then say the Our Father.
8. for each of the ten beads of the decade, next say ten Hail Marys while meditating on the Mystery.
9. Then say a Glory Be.
10. Fatima prayer
(11. repeat 6-10)

12. Hail Holy Queen
13. Final prayer
14. Sign of the cross
